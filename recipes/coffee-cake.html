CARROT CAKE RECIPES

This carrot coffee cake is a classic streusel coffee cake with a carrot cake profile. Nicely spiced and moist, with finely shredded carrots, walnuts, and a cream cheese glaze, it's just right for a spring brunch buffet.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup walnuts, chopped
  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter, melted

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. preheat oven
  3. prpare pan
  4. prepare carrots
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the dry streusel ingredients: flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Add the melted butter (and chopped nuts, if using).
  7. Mix with a fork or your fingers until the mixture is crumbly and resembles coarse sand with some larger clumps.
  8. Set aside, or refrigerate if your kitchen is warm, to allow the butter to firm up slightly. This helps prevent it from melting completely into the cake batter.
